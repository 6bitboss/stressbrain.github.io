StressBrain

Entrepreneurs are defined as individuals that creates and/or invests in one or more businesses.

They are often regarded as risk takers, leaders, and innovative souls. Often loved or hated by society, entreprenuers are always known as someone who works hard and takes risks that the average joe would not. The amount of stress creating and working with businesses is nearly astronomical.

But what is Stress?

Stress is a type of worry or mental tension which is caused by a difficult situtation. Often seen as a hindrance or mental problem, but thats not always true. Stress is a human responce that pushes us to deal with challenges in our lives. However, we often only start to worry about stress levels when they go much higher then they usually are, leading to different ailments.

Signs of Stress

Signs of stress usually entail:
  • Difficulty with relaxation
  • Increased irritability
  • Decrease or increases in appetite
  • Most notably, a cause/increase of mental health conditions such as depression and anxiety

Entrepreneurs and Mental Health

According to Visual Capitalist, entreprenuers have an increase in mental issues such as depression, ADHD, addiction and bipolar disorder.

Reports from show that between entreprenuers and average folk, 15% increase in depression, ADHD increase by 24%, addiction increased by 8%, and an increased chance to become bipolar (10% increase).
